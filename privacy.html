ChoreGo Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Who we are

ChoreGo is a family chore and allowance app. For support, see our support page or email seamus.j.browne@gmail.com.

Data we collect

We collect only what’s needed to run the app: email address (for sign-in), display name and role, and chore and payment data you create in the app.

How we use it

We use this data to provide the app. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Where it’s stored

We use Google Firebase to store and process this data. Firebase’s privacy practices apply.

Children

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what’s needed for the app. Parents can contact us to request deletion of their child’s data.

Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version will always be on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or to request deletion of your data: seamus.j.browne@gmail.com or our support page.
